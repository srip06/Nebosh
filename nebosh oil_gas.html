NEBOSH International Technical Certificate in Oil and Gas Operational Safety

  
 
   
 
 
 
 

The NEBOSH Oil and Gas Certificate is a contemporary qualification designed specifically for professionals with safety responsibilities in the oil and gas process industry. It is the latest addition to our portfolio of globally - recognized health, safety, environmental and risk management qualifications.
 

Who will take the NEBOSH technical certificate in Oil and Gas operational safety?

The NEBOSH international technical certificate in oil and gas is suitable for Managers, Supervisors, Employee representatives and newly appointed health and safety advisors based both in the UK and internationally, who have responsibility for ensuring general health and safety within the oil and gas industry as part of their day to day duties. The qualification focuses on international standards and management systems, enabling candidates to effectively discharge workplace health and safety responsibilities both onshore and offshore in the oil and gas industry worldwide.

 
 

What are the benefits for the organization?

The NEBOSH Oil and Gas Certificate will benefit national and international oil and gas companies working to international standards who are seeking to implement effective health and safety management across all areas of their operation. Ensuring that line managers within the oil and gas industry have a sound understanding of the principles of managing risk is a key building block of an effective safety culture. NEBOSH accredited course providers offer a variety of course formats enabling candidates and employers to arrange training around the needs of the business. Many NEBOSH training providers will offer in company courses, to allow the inclusion of material specifically relevant to the organization’s workplace.

 
 

What does the course cover?

The NEBOSH Oil and Gas Certificate cover’s the principles of health and safety management, and the identification and control of workplace hazards within the oil and gas industry. The syllabus takes a risk management approach based on best practice and international industry standards.

 
 

Where can I take a course leading to the NEBOSH Oil and Gas Certificate?

The NEBOSH Oil and Gas certificate is currently in its pilot phase and details of providers offering the course are available by contacting NEBOSH directly. NIST method of training involves Classroom at one of our sophisticated training venues or E-learning with paramount support which enables the learner to go at their own acceleration.

  
 

How is the course assessed?

The NEBOSH Oil and Gas certificate is assessed by a written examination of 100 marks and pass percentage is 45.

    Unit IOGC 1 : Management of International Oil and Gas Operational Safety

    Element 1: Health Safety and Environmental Management in Context
    Element 2: Hydrocarbon Process Safety 1
    Element 3: Hydrocarbon Process Safety 2
    Element 4: Fire Protection and Emergency Response
    Element 5: Logistics and Transport Operations
