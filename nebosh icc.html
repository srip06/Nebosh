NEBOSH International Certificate in Construction Health and Safety – U.K  
 
   
 
   
 
  The NEBOSH International Construction Certificate would be suitable for various levels of employees from the construction industry. Those managing this respective profession in a construction base should know the day to day duties and also should make the preparations prerequisite and confirm that the implications of their decisions are properly taken into account.
   
  NEBOSH International Construction Certificate course is for:

Professionals across the world who work in construction, civil engineering, infrastructure, utilities and more, and who need the right level of skills and knowledge in identifying and controlling common construction hazards to fulfill their health and safety responsibilities.

This course provides guidance to the professionals a comprehensive awareness on hazards and risks in their workplace and to progress the construction coordination in safe manner with systematic procedures.
 
   
  Benefits:

The Risks in a construction industry is more from low level to life threatening. The need of a safety officer having an excellent knowledge and talent in handling construction threats is a benefit for an industry and for the workers who work in that industry. This NEBOSH International Construction course makes those experts to be more proficiency at their work.

The International Construction Certificate course is the foundation for the ultimate goal for any HSE professionals that are pursuing International Diploma in NEBOSH.

It enhances recognition and financial benefits for existing HSE professionals.

Certificate in Construction Health and Safety know how to help employers avoid prosecution, litigation, absence costs and loss of reputation.

Certification in NEBOSH meets the academic requirements for Technician Membership (Tech IOSH) of Institution of Occupational Safety and Health (IOSH) and Associate membership (AIIRSM) of the International Institute of Risk & Safety Management (IIRSM).
 
     
 

  • Course Syllabus

    Unit IGC 1: Management of International Health & Safety

    •   Foundations in Health and safety
    •   Health and Safety management policy
    •   Health and safety management system-organizing
    •   Health and safety management system-planning
    •   Health and safety management system-measuring, audit and review

    Unit ICC1: Managing and controlling hazards in international construction activities

    •   Construction management
    •   Construction site hazards and control
    •   Manual and mechanical handling hazards and control
    •   Electrical Hazards and control
    •   Fire hazards and control
    •   Chemical and biological health hazards and control
    •   Physical, psychological health, hazards and control
    •   Working at heights-hazards and control
    •   Excavation work and confined spaces hazards and control
    •   Demolition - Hazards and control

    Unit ICC2: International construction health and safety practical application

    To carry out unaided safety inspection of a construction workplace, identifying more common hazards, deciding whether they are adequately controlled, suggesting appropriate cost effective remedial action, complete a report management regarding the inspections with recommendation.

  • Training Methodology

    E-Learning approach is well systematized; it lets you to ease and stretches your concentration to learn so that you will gain excellent knowledge over the subject.

  • Assessment Method

    NEBOSH International Construction certification assessment consists of two examinations (Unit I & Unit II) & a practical construction health and safety practical application.

  • Exemption

    International General Certificate (IGC1) is the core unit for all of the NEBOSH International Health and Safety Certificates, so to advance a NEBOSH Certificate you need to study IGC1 along with the applicable specialist unit and practical assessment. If you have been awarded a NEBOSH International Certification the last 5 years and wish to take another, you will be exempt from IGC1 and can simply add the specialist unit (ICC1) and practical assessment (ICC2) to achieve the new award.
